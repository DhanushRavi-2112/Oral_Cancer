About OralScan AI

Revolutionizing oral cancer detection through artificial intelligence

Our Mission

At OralScan AI, we're dedicated to improving early detection of oral cancer through cutting-edge artificial intelligence technology. Our dual-model approach combining VGG16 and RegNetY-320 architectures provides healthcare professionals with reliable, fast, and accurate diagnostic support.

Technology

Our platform leverages ensemble learning techniques, combining multiple deep learning models to achieve over 94% accuracy in oral cancer detection. The system processes images in under 30 seconds while maintaining the highest standards of medical data security and HIPAA compliance.

Impact

With over 50,000 images analyzed and partnerships with 100+ clinics worldwide, OralScan AI is making early detection more accessible and reliable for healthcare professionals globally.
